**Date**: March 23, 2026 | **Author**: Moritz Natalini | **Status**: Draft for Partner Review

## Executive Summary

March saw a **massive scale-up across all partners**, driven by Tradeit's EU/LATAM expansion (Mystery Box experiment) and Winline's continued tiered funnel approach. The universal pattern across all partners: **gaming missions convert at ~90%, but partner conversion missions are the critical bottleneck** at 1–2% completion.

**Key number**: 294K Tradeit users had rewards expire without claiming — the biggest single funnel leak across all partners.

## Partner-by-Partner Performance

### 1. Tradeit

#### March Highlights
- **16 campaign variants** launched (EU, CIS, LATAM geos) starting Mar 9–12, running through Mar 31
- **Mystery Box experiment** live on General missions — massive AL spike but lower downstream conversion rates

#### Mission Completion Rates

| Mission | Type | Users Joined | Completed | Completion % |
|---------|------|-------------|-----------|-------------|
| Play 1 game (EN) | Gaming | 678,524 | 596,965 | **87.6%** |
| Play 1 game (RU) | Gaming | 554,576 | 512,398 | **92.4%** |
| Trade any item (EN) | Partner | 583,298 | 14,198 | **2.4%** |
| Trade any item (RU) | Partner | 490,824 | 8,170 | **1.7%** |
| Trade $250+ (EN) | Partner | 20,472 | 1,242 | **6.1%** |
| Trade $250+ (RU high-value) | Partner | 815 | 25 | **3.1%** |
| Trade >20000 RUB (RU) | Partner | 28,017 | 364 | **1.3%** |

#### Key Findings — Tradeit
1. **Gaming → Partner conversion drop-off is 40x**: 90% play completion vs ~2% trade completion
2. **Mystery Box drove AL volume but diluted conversion quality**: Mar 9 week = 129K ALs (vs ~2.5K/week in Jan–Feb), but C1/C2 rates compressed
3. **High-value tier ($250+) self-selects engaged users**: 6.1% completion vs 2.4% for basic trade — these users are 2.5x more likely to convert
4. **Reward expiration leak**: 294K users with expired rewards, only 41K actually claimed. **7:1 expiration-to-claim ratio**

#### Recommendations for April — Tradeit
- **Add reward expiration reminders**: Push notification 24h and 2h before expiry. This is the lowest-hanging fruit — 294K users already completed missions but never claimed
- **Shorten time between mission completion and reward availability**: Reduce friction window where users lose intent
- **Keep tiered missions ($250+ tier)**: Self-selecting engaged users deliver 2.5x better conversion. Consider adding a mid-tier ($50–$100) to capture the gap
- **Evaluate Mystery Box ROI**: Volume up massively but downstream conversion diluted. Calculate cost-per-actual-trade vs control

### 2. Winline (Russia)

#### March Highlights

| Bet 25000 RUB @1.3 odds | Partner | 1 | 3,583 | 2,496 | **69.7%** |

#### Key Findings — Winline
1. **Tiered funnel works**: Once users pass account connection (1.7%), subsequent deposit/bet completion is 67–78%
2. **Account connection is the bottleneck** (same pattern as Tradeit's trade mission): 1.7% of 286K users = only 4,741 connected
3. **Deposit 3000 RUB (11.4%) outperforms Deposit 500 RUB (1.1%)**: Counter-intuitive — likely because the 3K tier is served to already-committed users further in the funnel, while 500 RUB goes to fresh users
4. **FTD + Bet missions generate 64–68% of total monthly value** (consistent with Jan/Feb pattern)

#### Recommendations for April — Winline
- **Focus optimization on account connection step**: This is the gate — 1.7% means 98.3% of interested users never enter the Winline ecosystem
- **Consider simplifying the account connection flow**: Can we deep-link directly into the Winline app registration with FACEIT SSO pre-filled?
- **Keep tiered deposit/bet structure**: The progressive commitment model shows strong late-funnel completion
- **Investigate Deposit 500 vs 3000 RUB disparity**: The 500 RUB tier underperforms — may need different targeting or a minimum viable commitment threshold

## Cross-Partner Patterns & Systemic Recommendations

### Universal Findings

| Pattern | Data Point | Impact |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| Gaming missions ~90% completion | All 5 partners, all geos | Gaming missions are reliable entry points — no changes needed |
| Partner conversion = 1–2% | Tradeit 2.4%, Winline 1.7%, WhiteMarket 0.9%, Winline_KZ 0.4% | **The #1 bottleneck across all partnerships** |
| Late-funnel completion is high | Winline 67–78%, Winline_KZ 99.7%, Tradeit $250+ at 6.1% | Users who convert once are likely to convert again |
| Reward leakage | Tradeit: 294K expired vs 41K claimed (7:1 ratio) | Massive value left on the table |
| Tiered missions work | Winline deposit→bet, Tradeit $250+ tier | Progressive commitment model validated |

### Systemic Recommendations for April (All Partners)

1. **Implement reward expiration notifications across all partners** — if Tradeit's 7:1 expiration ratio is representative, we're losing significant conversion value post-mission
2. **Add intermediate funnel steps** — the jump from "play a game" to "spend money on partner" is too steep. An intermediate action (browse partner site, create account, add to wishlist) would warm users up
3. **Standardise tiered missions** — progressive commitment (low → medium → high value) outperforms flat single-action missions
4. **Invest in the account connection UX** — Winline data proves that once users are in the partner ecosystem, late-funnel completion is 67–99%. The UX at the connection/registration step is the lever
5. **Re-engage BOFU users more aggressively** — WhiteMarket data shows BOFU at 79% of value. All partners should have a dedicated re-engagement mission for users who already converted once
