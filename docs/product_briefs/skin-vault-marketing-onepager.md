## What is Skin Vault?

Skin Vault is a new profile feature that displays a user's CS2 skin inventory directly on their FACEIT profile — with real-time market valuations powered by our partner Tradeit. Think of it as a "flex your collection" widget: users can showcase their rarest skins, see what their inventory is worth, and browse others' collections.

It turns the FACEIT profile from a stats page into a destination.

---

## Why it matters

1. **User engagement** — Skin Vault gives users a reason to visit profiles (their own and others'). In our 300-user beta, we saw **6,927 tab views from 4,734 unique users** in just 17 days — that's a 15:1 viewer-to-owner ratio. Users are actively browsing each other's inventories.

2. **Partner revenue** — Every Skin Vault interaction is a touchpoint with Tradeit's marketplace. Users who view skins see real prices and can click through to trade. This directly feeds our Tradeit partnership value (Account Linkage → KYC → First Trade → Repeat).

3. **Platform differentiation** — No other competitive gaming platform surfaces skin inventory natively on profiles. This is a unique reason for CS2 players to keep their FACEIT profile active.

4. **Monetisation surface** — Once adopted at scale, Skin Vault becomes inventory we can monetise: sponsored skin highlights, brand placements (e.g. "Skins by Tradeit"), and targeted offers based on what users own.

## The blocker: User consent

Skin Vault requires users to **opt in** — we need their explicit consent to access and display their Steam skin inventory on their FACEIT profile. This is a Privacy requirement (Legal review in progress).

This means adoption depends entirely on **how many users we can get to enable the feature**. Without a marketing push, organic discovery alone will limit reach — the beta proved there's demand, but users need to know the feature exists and actively turn it on.

---

## The ask

We're requesting a **dedicated B2C campaign** to drive Skin Vault opt-in adoption at scale. Here's what we envision:

### Campaign goals
1. **Awareness** — Let users know Skin Vault exists and what it does
2. **Activation** — Drive users to their profile settings to enable the feature
3. **Virality** — Encourage users to share their Skin Vault profiles (peer-to-peer discovery)

### Suggested channels & tactics

| Channel | Tactic | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **In-platform banner** | "Your skins, your profile — enable Skin Vault" CTA | Highest-intent audience, already logged in |
| **Email campaign** | Segmented push to CS2 active users (DAU/MAU) | Direct reach, can personalise by engagement tier |
| **Social (Twitter/X, Instagram)** | "Show us your Skin Vault" UGC campaign, showcase top collections | Drives virality + organic reach |
| **Discord** | Announcement in FACEIT official + community servers | Core community, high engagement |
| **Push notification** | One-time push to CS2 players on web/app | Immediate action driver |
| **Influencer/creator seeding** | Send Skin Vault previews to FACEIT creators | Social proof, aspiration ("look what s1mple's collection is worth") |

### What we'll provide from Product
- Feature screenshots / screen recordings for creative assets
- Key data points for messaging (e.g. "Users with Skin Vault get 15× more profile views")
- Landing page or deep link to the opt-in flow (once Privacy clears)
- A/B test support if Marketing wants to test messaging variants
- Live dashboard with adoption metrics so we can track campaign performance together
