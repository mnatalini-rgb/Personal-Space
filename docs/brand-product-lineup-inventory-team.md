## Quick Reference: Which Product for Which Brief?

| Advertiser says... | Recommend | Why |
|---|---|---|
| "We want user sign-ups / registrations" | **Mission + Account Linkage** | Verified account creation on partner platform. Proven funnel. |
| "We want deposits / purchases / KYC" | **Mission + CDP + Custom Tasks** | Full-funnel tracking with CRM integration. BOFU engagement. |
| "We want brand awareness / impressions" | **Brand Takeover** | 9–20 brand impressions per unique player per event. Ladder + match skin. |
| "We want video views" | **Brand Takeover + Rewarded Video** or **Play Interstitial** (coming soon) | Rewarded Video = high-attention, user-initiated. Play Interstitial = pre-match, 100% viewable. |
| "We want to reach gamers with specific hardware" | **Peripheral Targeting** (coming Q2) | First-party hardware data: target by brand, device type, CPU. |
| "We want skin traders / CS2 enthusiasts" | **Skin Vault + Tradeit** | CPC model on profile inventory display. Active experiment. |
| "We want display / banner ads" | **Display (Direct or Programmatic)** | Standard IAB formats. Direct or remnant via Prebid. |
| "We want video ads at scale" | **Outstream Video** | In-page video. Direct or remnant. |
| "We want a mix of performance + awareness" | **Brand Takeover + Mission combo** | Takeover for impressions, Mission for conversions. Can run together. |

## Product Detail Cards

### 1. Missions

**What it is**: Task-based competition where users complete in-game challenges to earn prizes. Users auto-join when they play a match.

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | 1:1 task-based competition |
| **Duration** | Typically 1 month |
| **Activation time** | 5 working days |
| **Primary KPI** | User acquisition + engagement (account linkages, conversions) |
| **Key features** | Account Linkage, Prize Expiration, CDP integration, Custom Tasks |
| **Exclusivity** | None — multiple missions from different advertisers can run simultaneously |

**Best for**: Performance advertisers who want verified user actions (sign-ups, KYC, deposits, purchases). Long-term partnerships, not one-off campaigns.

**What makes it unique**: Account Linkage eliminates code reselling — every lead is verified. CDP integration means we can track the full partner funnel and optimise targeting.

**Active partners**: Winline (Y3), Tradeit (Y2), WhiteMarket (Y3), PaySafe (signing)

---

### 2. Brand Takeover

**What it is**: Ladder-based branded competition with full visual takeover of the match experience.

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Ladder-based competition (individual & group) |
| **Duration** | 3 or 7 days |
| **Activation time** | 5 working days |
| **Primary KPI** | Brand Impressions (9–20 per unique player per event) |
| **Key features** | Drops, Account Linkage, Match Skin, Rewarded Video (in testing) |
| **Exclusivity** | **Geo-exclusive** — cannot run two Brand Takeovers in the same geo simultaneously |

**Best for**: Brand/agency clients who want high-frequency brand impressions in a sponsorship-like format. Easy to compare with traditional display for media buyers.

**Impression drivers**:
- Ladder participation = brand impressions per match
- Drops = +3 brand impressions per drop claimed
- Match Skin = branded visual during gameplay

**Active clients**: Logitech (4x Brand Takeover purchased, 7-day format, via Adam Goh). Logitech's agency has included FACEIT in their 2026 planning.

---

### 3. Drops

**What it is**: Add-on to Brand Takeover. Random post-match prize distribution.

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Distribution** | Random, post-match. Algorithm favours active players + broad distribution |
| **Claim window** | 12 hours. Unclaimed drops re-enter the pool |
| **Brand impressions** | 3 per drop claimed |
| **Top Tier Drops** | Rare, time-specific drops to incentivise daily play throughout the event |

**Not sold standalone** — always part of a Brand Takeover package.

### 4. Rewarded Video (NEW — in testing)

**What it is**: Short video ad shown at the Drop claim moment during a Brand Takeover. User watches the video to receive their prize.

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Trigger** | Drop claim during a Brand Takeover |
| **Format** | Video ad (short form) |
| **Status** | In testing with Logitech (3rd Brand Takeover, March 2026) |
| **Upsell** | Natural add-on to Brand Takeover — video on top of sponsorship |

**Best for**: Brand clients who want video delivery alongside a sponsorship package. High-attention moment — user has intent (claiming a prize).

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|

| **Frequency** | 1 per user per day |
| **Viewability** | 100% guaranteed |
| **Target users** | Free users only, solo players (Phase 1) |
| **Revenue potential** | ~$1.2M/year (video ads + incremental Premium conversions) |

**Rollout**: Phase 0 (A/B test, US) → Phase 1 (Tier 2 monetised) → Phase 2 (Tier 1) → Phase 3 (full)

### 8. Peripheral Targeting (COMING — Q2/Q3 2026)

**What it is**: First-party hardware data from FACEIT client users. Enables targeting by device brand, type, and CPU.

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Data collected** | CPU (brand, cores, speed), keyboard brand, mouse brand, device manufacturer |
| **Status** | Live in India (2,853 records analysed). Turkey (80.5K DAU) releasing this week |
| **Key finding** | 33.7% of records match gaming brands (Logitech 15.5%, Razer 12%, SteelSeries, Corsair) |
| **Target use case** | "Reach users who DON'T own a Logitech mouse" — conquest targeting for peripheral brands |
| **Rollout** | India → Turkey → Tier 2 EU → Tier 1 (gated on privacy review) |

**Best for**: Endemic peripheral/hardware brands (Logitech, Razer, SteelSeries, Corsair, HyperX). Enables targeting and incrementality measurement that standard display can't offer.

**⚠️ Not yet available for sale. Data validation in progress. Privacy review required before Tier 1.**

## Engagement Engine (Cross-Product Feature)

Not a standalone product — it's a re-engagement system that works across Missions.

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **What it does** | Sends AI-generated push notifications to users stuck on mission tasks |
| **Best frameworks** | Progress Completion (10.6% CTR) and Urgency (9.4% CTR) |
| **Impact** | ~+0.2pp uplift in task completions. BOFU retention tool, not acquisition |

Relevant for briefs that include ongoing engagement or retention KPIs.

## Account Linkage & CDP (Cross-Product Infrastructure)

These aren't products you sell — they're capabilities that make Missions and Brand Takeover more valuable.

**Account Linkage**: Creates a verified 1:1 relationship between FACEIT user and partner platform. Eliminates code reselling. Gates prize eligibility.

**CDP**: Stores enriched user data from partner CRM (registration, KYC, deposits, purchases). Enables funnel segmentation and targeted missions.

**When to mention in briefs**: Any performance advertiser who wants verified actions or funnel tracking. Key differentiator vs standard gaming media buys.
