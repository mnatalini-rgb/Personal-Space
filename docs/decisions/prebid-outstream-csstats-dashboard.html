Head-to-Head: Mobalytics vs Primis

Mobalytics outperforms Primis on the primary metric RPV by +42%. Despite serving 5.4x fewer impressions, Mobalytics generated 45.8% more revenue through dramatically higher eCPM ($0.31 vs $0.04). However, the US anomaly (284 Primis impressions vs 98K Mobalytics) means the comparison is skewed — Mobalytics' US revenue ($45.37, 67.6% of total) has no Primis equivalent.

Anomalies & Caveats

  • 🇺🇸 US Gap: GA page views are balanced (Moba 2,610 vs Primis 2,444), yet Primis serves virtually nothing (284 imp vs 98K). This is a Primis supply-side fill issue, not a traffic split problem. Mobalytics' US revenue ($45.37) inflates the comparison. Excluding US: Moba $21.77 vs Primis $45.97 — Primis wins ex-US.
  • 📅 Date mismatch: Moba data is Mar 30 – Apr 8 (10 days). Primis is aggregated Mar 30 – Apr 10 (12 days). Not perfectly aligned.
  • 🔢 Impression volume: Primis fills 5.4x more impressions at much lower eCPM. Volume strategy vs premium strategy.

5. Experiment Decision Framework

Key Questions

Is the traffic split valid?

Yes. The Mobalytics vs Primis comparison is balanced at 50.6% vs 49.4% (Δ 2.6%) when isolating the two variants.

Which variant delivers higher RPV?

Mobalytics RPV $0.028 vs Primis RPV $0.020 — Mobalytics leads by 42%. BUT this is driven by US where Primis has no fill. Ex-US, Primis wins on total revenue.

Are there geo-specific performance variations?

Yes. Mobalytics dominates in US ($0.46 eCPM, 67.6% of revenue). Primis leads in Germany ($13.19 vs $4.22), Norway ($2.11 vs n/a), and has much higher fill globally.
