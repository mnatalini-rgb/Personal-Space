### Qualitative Update — What shipped in Q1 2026

**Major initiatives:**
1. **Mystery Box Experiment (March)**
   - Deployed across 4 regions (CIS, NA, MENA, APAC) for General Mission segment
   - Impact: AL rates increased dramatically — CIS: 1.9-2.5% → 13.6%, MENA: 5.3-6.1% → 22.3%, NA: 5.4% → 7.7%, APAC: 3.0-3.5% → 10.6%
   - Trade-off: C1 rate (% of AL) declined — expected, as broader/less-qualified audience now linking
   - KL: Gamification via Mystery Box is the strongest AL lever we've tested. Volume vs. quality trade-off needs monitoring at C1/C2 level

2. **Peripheral Expansion — Tier 3 Rollout**
   - Expanded from TR+IN+IT pilot to all 90 Tier 3 countries in single batch
   - Rationale: Low marginal cost, fills rate already competitive, regulatory risk minimal
   - KL: Batch rollout more efficient than phased — monitoring for eCPM dilution

3. **Play Interstitial Business Case**
   - Built comprehensive model (185 countries × tier × CPM × fill rate)
   - Mid-scenario: [revenue figure from spreadsheet]
   - Ready for commercial team review and experiment design
   - KL: Free-user-only targeting preserves subscriber experience while unlocking ~37M annual sessions

4. **Publift Optimization (ongoing)**
   - Replaced Ezoic in GAM waterfall with Publift prebid
   - Performance: significant eCPM improvement driven by latency reduction
   - KL: Ad tech performance is latency-bound, not demand-bound on our inventory

5. **Partner Portfolio Growth**
   - PaySafe onboarded as new partner
   - Tradeit March spike: €68.7K (vs. €12-13K in Jan/Feb) driven by new regions + Mystery Box
   - Winline stable at €164-204K/month

**Quick Wins (smaller impactful ideas):**
- **Trust Modal A/B Test:** Keeping the modal doubles AL rate (5.34% vs 2.75%, p < 0.001). Simple UX decision, massive impact.
- **Mission Chain (sequential segment):** +1.96pp uplift (94.7% relative improvement) — users engage more when missions unlock progressively
- **Engagement Engine reminders:** +0.21pp uplift — psychological framing matters, especially for already-engaged users
