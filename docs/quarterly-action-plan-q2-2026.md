## How This Plan Works

## Theme 1: Radical Focus (Use NSM to Say No)

**William's feedback**: Use the TAVI framework to prioritise ruthlessly. Say no to work that doesn't move OVU or AVU.

### Actions

| # | Action | Success Criteria | Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1.1 | Every new initiative gets a TAVI impact assessment before committing eng resources | Written 1-paragraph NSM connection in every PRD and one-pager | April onwards |
| 1.2 | Decline or deprioritise at least 2 requests per month that don't have a clear TAVI path | Log declined requests with reasoning in a "said no" tracker | April onwards |
| 1.3 | Run a quarterly prioritisation review using TAVI as the ranking criterion | Produce a ranked initiative list with TAVI impact scores shared with William | End of June |
| 1.4 | Get NSM tracking operational — 30-day rolling Total Partner Value and AVU data flowing | Retool dashboard or BigQuery scheduled query producing daily rolling numbers | End of April |

### Monthly Checkpoints

- **April**: NSM tracking live (30-day rolling). First 2 "said no" entries logged. All active PRDs have TAVI connection paragraph.
- **May**: TAVI data informing at least 1 prioritisation decision (documented). "Said no" tracker has 4+ entries.
- **June**: Quarterly prioritisation review completed. Share ranked list with William. Reflect on whether saying no freed capacity for higher-impact work.

## Theme 3: Managing Tension (Let Go of Heat)

**William's feedback**: Stop engaging in heated debates. Diffuse with data. Operate at Principal/Director communication level.

### Actions

| # | Action | Success Criteria | Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| 3.1 | When disagreement arises in a meeting, default response: "Let me pull the data and come back by [time]" | Zero heated exchanges in meetings for 4+ consecutive weeks | April onwards |
| 3.2 | For contentious topics, prepare a 1-page data brief before the meeting — not during | At least 2 pre-prepared briefs per month for known-contentious topics | April onwards |
| 3.3 | After any tense interaction, write a private debrief note: what triggered it, what would have worked better | Private log with at least 1 entry per month (hopefully fewer over time) | April onwards |
| 3.4 | Identify 1-2 recurring friction points (people, topics, meetings) and develop a pre-planned approach for each | Written "playbook" for the top 2 friction scenarios | End of April |

### Monthly Checkpoints

- **April**: Friction playbook written. First data-brief-before-meeting completed. Debrief log started.
- **May**: Check: any heated exchanges this month? If yes, what was the trigger vs. the playbook? If no, the system is working.
- **June**: Review debrief log. Pattern recognition: are the same triggers recurring? Adjust playbook. Ask William for feedback on communication tone.

## Quarterly Scoring (End of June Self-Assessment)

| Theme | Weight | How to Score |
|---|---|---|
| 1. Radical Focus | 20% | Is TAVI tracking live? Are you saying no with data? |
| 2. Communicate & Evangelise | 25% | Post count, slide quality, proactive sharing with Commercial |
| 3. Managing Tension | 15% | Zero heated exchanges? Data-first responses? |
| 4. Executive Influence | 20% | Director+ relationships built? Invited to strategic discussions? |
| 5. Vertical Expansion | 20% | Verticals mapped? Audience packages built? Case study done? |

**Scoring scale:**
- **5** — Exceeded: all actions complete + measurable impact observed
- **4** — Met: all actions complete
- **3** — Mostly met: 75%+ actions complete
- **2** — Partially met: 50% actions complete
- **1** — Missed: <50% actions complete

**Target: average score of 3.5+ across all themes.**
