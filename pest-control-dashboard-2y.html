Recommendations for RTM Direct Action

  • Immediate Fixes: Send a handyman to repair the damaged bin store mesh and fit steel plates to the bottom of the gas cupboard door. Seal all gnawed holes between the two rooms.
  • Waste Management: Send strict communications to residents regarding bin lids. The unlimited food supply in the bin store is the primary driver of the rat population.
  • Deep Clean: Arrange a professional deep clean of the bin store and gas cupboard to remove old droppings and nesting materials, making new activity easier to spot.
  • Pest Strategy: Authorise BioPest to deploy the recommended intensive cholecalciferol rodenticide programme, given the ineffectiveness of snap traps on the current population.
