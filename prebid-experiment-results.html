Methodology & Constraints

Experiment Setup

  • Platform: CSStats.gg (csstats.gg) — 1.2M daily users, 9.3M MAU
  • A/B split: 50/50 via GA experiment (ads_ab_variant: "prebid" vs "publift")
  • Period: 25 Feb – 16 Mar 2026 (20 days)
  • Page views: Moba cohort 3.59M / Publift cohort 3.52M (excl. Russia)

Key Decisions

  • Russia excluded: GAM does not operate in Russia — 2.6M views excluded from both cohorts
  • Fill rate rejected as metric: Publift maintains a GAM placeholder line item for unfilled impressions, inflating fill rate to ~100%. Revenue per Page View is the only reliable comparison.
  • Publift commission: ~20% revenue share deducted to show net revenue to publisher
  • US data anomaly: Analysis presented both including and excluding US due to 234x revenue discrepancy requiring investigation

Data Sources

  • GA4 page views by experiment cohort and geo
  • GAM revenue report: Moba ad units (23318403314, 23318404274)
  • GAM revenue report: Publift ad unit (23328749198)
  • Market tier classification (Tier 1: 8, Tier 2: 33, Tier 3: 90 countries)

Recommendation

Migrate to Moba (Prebid) with phased rollout

  1. Investigate US anomaly — Verify GAM ad unit configuration and demand partner setup for US traffic before final decision. This does not block the rollout for non-US markets.
  2. Phase 1: Migrate Tier 3 markets — Moba shows +27.5% advantage. Low revenue risk, high relative gain. Target: immediate.
  3. Phase 2: Migrate Tier 2 markets — +12.0% advantage. Monitor for 1 week, confirm eCPM stability. Target: +1 week.
  4. Phase 3: Migrate Tier 1 markets (excl. US) — +11.5% advantage. Highest absolute revenue markets; confirm demand partner coverage. Target: +2 weeks.
  5. Phase 4: US migration — Pending anomaly resolution. If Moba's $10.75 eCPM is legitimate, US is the strongest case for migration.

Financial impact estimate: Based on this 20-day sample (excl. Russia, excl. US), switching to Moba would yield ~$63.69 additional revenue vs Publift net ($441.99 Moba vs $378.30 Publift net). Annualised across CSStats alone, this represents ~$1,163 incremental revenue — before applying to FACEIT's larger inventory.
