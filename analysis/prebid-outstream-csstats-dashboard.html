Executive Summary

Experiment Concluded — Moba Wins

Verdict: Mobalytics Wins (GREEN) — +90% net RPV

Over the full 25-day test (Mar 30 – Apr 23), Mobalytics outperformed Primis on the primary metric Revenue Per Page View by +90% ($0.038 vs $0.020 per 1K views, both net). The gap is driven by US fill — Moba serves 253K US impressions vs Primis 499 — but Moba also wins overall even after accounting for Primis's higher ex-US volume. Recommendation: ship Mobalytics on CSStats and run the same A/B test on FACEIT.com to validate at scale.

Head-to-Head: Mobalytics vs Primis

Mobalytics outperforms Primis on the primary metric RPV by +90%. Despite serving 5.3x fewer impressions, Moba generated 88% more revenue through dramatically higher eCPM ($0.36 vs $0.037). The US fill gap is the key driver — Moba's US revenue ($125.22, 68% of total) has no Primis equivalent (Primis: $0.15 US). Both revenues are reported net. Verdict: Mobalytics wins clearly.

Key Observations

  • 🇺🇸 US Fill Gap (key driver): Moba 253,371 US impressions ($125.22) vs Primis 499 ($0.15). This is a Primis supply-side fill issue, not a traffic split problem — GA page views are balanced. US revenue = 68% of Moba total.
  • 🌍 Ex-US: Primis $97.61 vs Moba ~$59. Primis wins ex-US by ~$39, but the US gap ($125 vs $0) overwhelms this. A hybrid (Moba US + Primis RoW) saves ~$39/month — not worth the operational complexity.
  • 💰 Both net: Both Primis and Moba revenues are reported net. No additional fees to deduct — the comparison is like-for-like.
  • 🔢 Impression strategy: Primis fills 5.3x more impressions at much lower eCPM ($0.037 vs $0.36). Volume can't compensate for the eCPM/fill gap.

5. Experiment Decision Framework

Key Questions — Answered

Is the traffic split valid?

Yes. Balanced at 50.15% vs 49.85% (Δ 0.6%) when isolating the two variants. 25 days of data.

Which variant delivers higher RPV?

Mobalytics: $0.038 RPV vs Primis $0.020 RPV — Moba wins by +90%. Driven by US fill but Moba also wins on overall net revenue ($184 vs $98).

Are there geo-specific performance variations?

Yes. Moba dominates US ($125 vs $0.15). Primis leads ex-US by ~$19 net. Hybrid approach not worth the complexity for ~$19/month delta.
