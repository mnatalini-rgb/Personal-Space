AL Growth — Trust Modal Redesign

Increasing account linkage rates through trust signal optimisation

Owner: Moritz Natalini · Team: Anouk (Design), Teodor (FE) · Jira: AD-3946

1. Background & Hypothesis

Account Linkage (AL) is the entry point to the partner conversion funnel: AL → KYC (C1) → First Deposit (C2) → Deeper Milestones (C3). The volume and quality of ALs directly determine partner value measured in € per 1K EBU.

The Trust Modal experiment (concluded March 2026) proved that showing a FACEIT trust badge before the partner login screen doubles the linkage-step completion rate. Users need reassurance before sharing credentials with third-party partners.

3. Design Brief for Anouk

Objective

Redesign the trust modal to further improve AL completion rate. Build on the proven trust signal concept — the current modal already doubles completion. The design challenge: can we do even better?

Current UX flow

  1. User is shown a mission/event requiring account linkage
  2. User clicks to link account
  3. Trust modal appears with: partner branding (logo + name), benefits list (Hassle free, Automated, Safety & Security), and "CONNECT ACCOUNT" CTA
  4. User clicks CTA → redirected to partner login/OAuth

What the current modal shows

  • Partner logo + verified badge
  • Headline: "Connect your account once to automatically claim prizes from this organizer of all missions and events"
  • Benefits list with checkmarks: "Hassle free — One time login for all FACEIT competitions", "Automated — Claim any prize without copy and paste codes", "Safety & Security — No personal data shared with partners"
  • Orange CTA button: "CONNECT ACCOUNT"

Design constraints

  • MUST keep trust signals (FACEIT verified badge, security messaging)
  • MUST show partner branding (logo, name)
  • MUST include clear CTA
  • CAN experiment with: layout, copy, visual hierarchy, benefit framing, social proof elements, progress indicators
  • SHOULD explore: A/B testable variants (min 2 designs)
